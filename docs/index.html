YeastGenome Android

[Stanford University Cherry Lab] [Summer 2014]

[GitHub Repository]

YeastGenome is a hybrid iPhone/Android application that recreates many features in the previous, antiquated iPhone/iPad YeastGenome application. YeastGenome allows a user to search and browse 8 MB of in-app local data, and search and favorite genes and chromosomal features. YeastGenome uses Cordova, OnsenUI, and AngularJS. The first app I ever built, YeastGenome was created over 4 weeks in Summer 2014 and released to the Google Play Store 4 weeks later. This app is no longer maintained or available on the Google Play Store.
